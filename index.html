Understanding Body Mass Index

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a rapid biological indicator scaling your body mass relative to your stature. While not diagnostic of absolute vascular health, it provides a crucial baseline standard across demographics.

< 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Normal Weight
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
≥ 30.0 Obese
Core Equation (Metric System) BMI = Weight (kg) / Height² (m²)
